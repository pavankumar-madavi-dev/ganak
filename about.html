About Ganak

Free, accurate calculators — built for anyone, anywhere.

Our Mission

Ganak was built to make everyday calculations — loans, savings, construction costs, age, and more — simple and accessible without needing an app, a sign-up, or a fee. Whether you're planning a home loan, budgeting your month, or estimating a building project, Ganak gives you a fast, reliable answer.

Who Built This

Ganak is developed and maintained by niDar Marketing And Services, an MSME-registered firm based in Sadak Arjuni, Gondia, Maharashtra, India. We build mobile-first digital tools and platforms for everyday use.

Why Free?

We believe useful tools shouldn't sit behind a paywall. Ganak is supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertising so that every calculator stays free for everyone, everywhere in the world.
