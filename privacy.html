Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Ganak's calculators run entirely in your browser — numbers you enter (loan amount, date of birth, income, etc.) are never sent to or stored on our servers. Calculations happen locally on your device.

2. Anonymous Site Analytics

We use a basic visitor counter to see how many people use the site each day. This does not identify you personally — it only counts page visits.

3. Comments

If you choose to leave a comment, it is handled through GitHub Discussions (Giscus) and requires a GitHub account. That data is governed by GitHub's own privacy policy.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies beyond what may be required by third-party services (such as the visitor counter or, in future, Google AdSense) shown on this page.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external services (Telegram, WhatsApp, exchange platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.

6. Children's Privacy

Ganak is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via our Contact Us page.
